Our Capabilities

Gusset Plates

Engineered for multi-directional load paths, our gusset plates offer rigid, high-strength connections between beams, braces, and columns. Designed for structural integrity under seismic and dynamic conditions.

Embed Plates

Precision-cut steel plates with integrated studs or anchors, embedded into concrete to support steel connections with dependable anchorage and load transfer.

Base Plates

Fabricated for accurate anchor bolt placement and load-bearing stability, our base plates ensure secure column-to-foundation connections.

Stiffener Plates

Our stiffeners reduce web and flange deformation, reinforcing key stress points across steel beams and columns for enhanced structural resilience.

Shear Tabs

These welded tabs deliver efficient field connections that transfer shear forces while minimizing installation time and labor costs.

Lintels

Durable steel lintels designed to support masonry above openings like doors and windows. Precision-cut for strength and easy installation.

Bollards

High-strength steel bollards built for impact resistance and perimeter protection. Ideal for traffic control and securing entry points.

Angle Brackets

Versatile angle brackets that align and secure structural components in custom framing, decking, and miscellaneous steel assemblies.

About Us

GSBS Fabrication is a family-owned steel shop built on years of hands-on expertise and unwavering dedication to quality. We specialize in the high-precision fabrication of structural steel components that are often overlooked—but are absolutely essential to the strength, stability, and safety of any build. From shear tabs and stiffener plates to custom embeds and connection assemblies, we deliver mission-critical parts with unmatched accuracy and consistency. Our shop is trusted by leading general contractors, structural engineers, and commercial fabricators alike for our craftsmanship, responsiveness, and code-compliant results. At GSBS, we don’t just fabricate steel—we reinforce the backbone of every structure we touch.
