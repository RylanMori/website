Our Capabilities

Connection Plates

Our connection plates are engineered for durability and precision. Each piece is cut and drilled to exact specifications to ensure seamless integration into structural systems. These plates facilitate secure connections between beams and columns, supporting the integrity of your entire framework.

Base & Embed Plates

We fabricate custom base and embed plates that serve as the foundational connection between steel structures and concrete. Every plate is precision-machined with exact hole placement and reinforced for long-term structural performance in commercial and industrial settings.

Stiffener Plates

Our stiffener plates are designed to resist local buckling and improve load distribution across beams and columns. Whether interior or exterior flange reinforcement is required, we fabricate to meet both standard and custom engineering demands with dependable accuracy.

Shear Tabs

GSBS shear tabs are built to handle high-stress load transfers between structural members. We precision-cut every tab for ease of field installation, enabling quicker build times without compromising connection strength or safety standards.

Bent Plates

Our bent plates are formed using CNC-controlled press brakes to maintain consistent bend radii and tolerance control. Whether for stair stringers, coping, or custom load-transfer applications, we deliver flawless bends in every run.

Angle Clips

GSBS angle clips are tailored for structural and miscellaneous framing requirements. We cut and drill clips to fit exact connection points, providing the flexibility needed for a variety of field applications, from joist hangers to curtain wall supports.
